About Us

A hub of over 10,000 questions and answers to give every student a chance at exam sucess.

Practicequestions.ng , a module of eschool ng, an online learning and school management platform, was created from the need to adequately prepare students for their upcoming examinations.Through the help and knowledge of certified teachers,we have been able to create a database of over 10,000 clear and detailed questions and answers.

Our practice questions are time based ,multiple choice and automatically graded after the completion of an exam. We currently boast of having a vast number of question types which cuts across Junior and Senior Secondary subjects.


Practice questions are beneficial to any student preparing for exams because they broaden your view of knowledge and expose you to possible exam questions. As we all know practice makes perfect, start your journey of good grades with practice questions
